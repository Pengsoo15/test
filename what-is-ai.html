Artificial Intelligence (AI) refers to computer systems designed to perform tasks that typically require human intelligence. These tasks include learning from experience, recognizing patterns, understanding language, and making decisions.

AI systems can analyze massive amounts of data, identify trends, and provide insights far faster than humans. From virtual assistants to medical diagnosis tools, AI is transforming how we live and work.

Modern AI encompasses various approaches, including machine learning, deep learning, natural language processing, and computer vision. Each approach solves different types of problems and has unique applications across industries.
